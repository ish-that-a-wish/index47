Apex Pathing
Apex | Apex Pathing is versatile and highly compatible with other libraries. The goal is to support a variety of path following algorithms, allowing users to mix and match different systems to perfect their autonomous routine. Some types of following systems include but are not limited to: quintic splines, cubic splines, P2P pathing. They also aim to develop for wide range of drivetrains and localizers.
