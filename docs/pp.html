PP | Title of section (PP | Pedro Pathing) Pedro Pathing is one of the most widely used autonomous path planners in FIRST Tech Challenge. It is developed by a multitude of teams themselves and is widely praised for being efficient and intuitive. This program allows teams to draw their own paths using a visualizer and export it to code that the robot can follow. It also has support for various drivetrains including mecanum, coaxial swerve, differential swerve, and many others. In addition, it supports the use of multiple types of localizer, such as goBILDA pinpoint, two wheel odometry, and three wheel odometry. A recent update added a new command based system as well, which lets teams control the rest of their subsystems with ease. This gives teams more options in terms of how they want to program their autonomous.
