About Index47

Index47 was started in May of 2026, by programmers on 13100 47 Beavers. This was started through observations that many people in the FTC community are not always caught up with references, definitions, or acronyms. This website simply solves all of these problems!

How to use

Type a keyword or acronym into the search bar (e.g. pid or swerve), click a result to open its page, and hit ← Return to come back. Toggle light/dark mode with the ◐ button. Your preference is saved.
